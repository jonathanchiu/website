Time

In progress

Calculates time dilation experienced by a traveling person from the perspective of an observer at "rest", and vice-versa, given a duration of travel and a speed at which the moving observer desires to travel at. Will determine how much time has passed for both people utilizing the lorentz factor.

Time dilation is really cool and incredibly bizarre.
